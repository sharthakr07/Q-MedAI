QMedAI fuses classical machine learning, IBM Qiskit quantum kernels, and SHAP explainability into a single diagnostic pipeline — giving clinicians predictions they can trust and trace.

Three-layer diagnostic engine

Each prediction passes through all three stages. Every output is traceable.

01
Classical ML
Risk classification
A gradient boosted classifier processes patient vitals, history, and lifestyle data to produce an initial risk profile and confidence estimate.
02
Quantum ML
Kernel augmentation
IBM Qiskit's QSVC and VQC models re-evaluate the feature vector on a quantum kernel, surfacing non-linear patterns classical models miss.
03
Explainable AI
SHAP + LIME analysis
Every prediction is explained at the feature level — clinicians see which biomarkers drove the result; patients receive a plain-language summary.
